ABOUT MUNNAR

  • It is a hill station in Kerala, located about 1,600 metres above sea level in the Western Ghats.
  • The area is famous for sprawling tea plantations and colonial-era bungalows.
  • Eravikulam National Park, home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr and the high peak Anamudi, is one of its major attractions.
  • The Tea Museum (or Tata / KDHP Tea Museum) showcases historical tea processing machinery and exhibits about tea cultivation.
  • Top Station offers panoramic views of the Western Ghats, mist-covered valleys, and on clear days views of the plains of Tamil Nadu.
  • Mattupetty Dam and Lake provide boating, lush surroundings, and peaceful spots for picnics.
  • The climate is cool and refreshing, and the scenery includes misty valleys, lush greenery, waterfalls, and rare flora like the Neelakurinji (which blooms periodically).
