ABOUT VEGAMON

  • Vagamon is a hill station located on the border of Kottayam and Idukki districts, at about 1,100 meters above sea level.
  • The scenery features rolling grasslands/meadows, pine forests, tea plantations, winding valleys and meadows.
  • It is well known for eco-tourism and adventure-activities such as trekking, rock climbing, paragliding and mountaineering.
  • Vagamon also has religious/spiritual significance: there are three hills named Thangal Hill, Murugan Hill, and Kurisumala, which are important for different faiths.
  • The Vagamon Glass Bridge, opened in 2023, is a prominent attraction—it's a cantilever glass bridge built with five layers of thick glass, steel-supported.
  • The climate is cool and pleasant; summers see mild temperatures, winters are colder, and monsoon season brings lush greenery and frequent rains.
  • It's about 64 km from Kottayam; the nearest major railway station is Kottayam, and the nearest airport is Kochi International Airport.
